About Eye Disease Detection Using Deep Learning

SmartEye is a project focused on detecting eye diseases using cutting-edge deep learning techniques. Our AI model is trained to classify images into four categories: Normal, Cataract, Diabetic Retinopathy, and Glaucoma. This assists in early diagnosis and helps patients receive timely treatment.
